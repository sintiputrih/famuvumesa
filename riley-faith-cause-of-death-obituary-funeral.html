Riley Faith cause of death, obituary, funeral

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, July 28, 2024

This sad report will focus on Riley Faith’s cause of death, obituary and funeral arrangements her family is making in the wake of the devastating news of her death.

Faith is a 7-year-old girl who lost her battle with cancer on Saturday night. Riley Faith was just four years old when she received the diagnosis of adrenal carcinoma.

The malignancy spread even after numerous clinical trials, chemotherapy, and different medications. Her family reported that she was in a great deal of discomfort because it was in her lungs and covered part of her liver.

Riley Faith cause of death, obituary, funeral

As previously mentioned, Riley Faith, with much life ahead of her died of cancer. Her family and people who knew her are utterly devasted by the news. Obituary from all angles has flooded social media in her honour.

Shore Up shared the sad story on Facebook writing;

“This is Riley Faith.

Last night I found myself reading her story. She loved to paint, play outside, and do all the things little kids love to do.

The difference between Riley and other little kids is Riley was diagnosed with adrenal cancer at the age of 4. And for 3 years she fought.

This morning I woke to the news that Riley had passed away.

Kids shouldn’t get sick. Whether it’s cancer or something else. Kids should not get sick. Death is inexplicably difficult. And the death of a child magnifies the darkness of this or any disease even more.

If you are a person of prayer and faith, please pray for Riley’s family. Their grief is real and life will never be the same for them.

The following is what was posted on Riley Faith’s page this morning.

Riley Faith is out of pain. Riley is in heaven with Jesus. She passed away at home last night. She was surrounded by family. We miss her so much, and our hearts are broken.

Forever Seven and Forever in our Hearts

We love you Riley

May 6, 2016 – July 22, 2023″

Funeral arrangements for the late Riley Faith will be communicated to the general public by the family very soon. Keep reading for more updates.
