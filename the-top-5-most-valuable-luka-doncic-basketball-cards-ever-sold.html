The Top 5 Most Valuable Luka Doncic Basketball Cards Ever Sold

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, February 21, 2024

I have been a big time sports fan and card collector my whole life, so obviously this market for basketball cards is just exploding with the sales of rare and valuable crds going into hundreds of millions at some cases. Among the NBA top players, it certainly also left a mark on card sales for Luka Doncic now constantly progressing Slovenian of Dallas Mavericks. So in this article, we take a look at the top 5 most expensive basketball card sales with Luka Doncic and examine what drives their mind-boggling price tags.


The Rise of NBA Superstar Luka Doncic


But before we get to the specifics of which Luka Doncic's basketball card has fetched how much money, first a brief history lesson on why his proverbial stock has risen so quickly since being drafted into the league. Luka Doncic was his new European sensation and the league slightly readjusted their priorities in order to get ready for him once he came stateside. The spectacular debut that saw him tally 21.2 points, 7.8 rebounds, and a league-rookie-best six assists per game not only earned Simmons the Rookie of the year award at end-of-season functions throughout but also made clear he was one of its top young talents early into his career.

The Top 5 Most Expensive Luka Doncic Basketball Card Sales

  • 2018 Panini National Treasures Luka Doncic 1/1 Logoman Autograph

    • Sale Price: $4.6 million 
    • Date of Sale: February 28, 2021
    • The card was initially sold for a staggering $4.6 million, making it the most expensive basketball card ever sold at the time.

  • 2018 Panini National Treasures Luka Doncic 1/1 Logoman Autograph

    • Sale Price: $3.12 million 
    • Date of Sale: November 17, 2022
    • Despite a considerable drop from its previous sale, this unique card's rarity and value continue to command hefty sums in the market.

  • 2018 Panini National Treasures Luka Dončić Logoman Autograph Rookie Card #127

    • Highest Sale Price: $4.6 million
    • This rare card features the Slovenian star player Luka Doncic during his rookie season with the Dallas Mavericks. Its scarcity and association with a rising NBA star significantly contribute to its high value.

  • 2003 Upper Deck Exquisite Collection LeBron James Rookie Patch Autograph

    • Sale Price: $5.2 million 
    • Date of Sale: April 26, 2021
    • Though not solely focused on Luka Doncic, this sale demonstrates the immense value of basketball cards and the extraordinary prices collectors are willing to pay for rare and iconic pieces.

  • 2003-04 Upper Deck Exquisite Collection LeBron James Rookie Patch Autograph

    • Sold for $2.1 million 
    • Date of Sale: 2022
    • Another remarkable sale of a LeBron James card that reflects the ongoing trend of escalating values in the basketball card market.

    • Factors Contributing to the High Value of Luka Doncic Cards

    The high value of Luka Doncic basketball cards can be attributed to several key factors: Cards such as the 2018 Panini National Treasures Luka Doncic 1/1 Logoman Autograph are one-of-a-kind, making them exceptionally rare and desirable for collectors. Luka Doncic's remarkable performance on the court has significantly enhanced the appeal of his cards, especially during his rookie season. The growing interest of investors and high-profile individuals, such as Drake, in acquiring rare basketball cards has further fueled the market's upward trajectory.

    In the End

    The world of basketball card sales continues to captivate enthusiasts, collectors, and investors alike, with record-breaking sales and unprecedented values. Luka Doncic's rise to NBA stardom has not only elevated his status as a top player but has also contributed to the soaring prices of his rare and valuable basketball cards. As the market evolves and new records are set, the allure of these iconic pieces of sports memorabilia only continues to grow, making them a thrilling and lucrative aspect of the sports world.
