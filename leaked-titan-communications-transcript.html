Leaked Titan Communications Transcript: Passengers Knew About Fault

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 6, 2024

A leaked Titan Communications Transcript has given rise to some significant concerns regarding the tragic events of the submersible. 

The leaked document shows that the passengers on board knew something was wrong with the vessel 18 minutes before it sank. 

While rumors regarding the authenticity of the transcript were doing the rounds, an alleged authentic document has surfaced recently.

Following the recent leak, there is a growing curiosity to uncover the details of the transcript and its implications. Based on the claims made in the transcript, it is asserted that passengers had become aware of an issue with the submersible 18 minutes before their tragic demise.

The communications between the submersible and its mother, Polar Prince, were allegedly captured and leaked. 

The leaked Titan Communications Transcript document reveals that the passengers were aware of the malfunction before the submersible sank.

The Role of James Cameron

The leaked Titan Communications Transcript reportedly mentioned remarks made by James Cameron, the director of the hit movie Titanic. 

Many believed that Cameron’s remarks were based on the transcripts. This information led to even more interest in finding out more about the content of the supposedly leaked documents. 

However, it was later confirmed that the initial documents circulated on Telegram were fake.

The authenticity of leaked Titan Communications Transcript

On the 14th of July, an alleged authentic copy of the same leaked document appeared online. 

There is speculation that the document under discussion was indeed the same one, however, it had limited distribution. 

The authentic version of the document has raised additional concerns about the tragedy and whether it could have been preventable had the passengers been informed about the flaws in the submersible.

The cause of accident

It is still unknown what caused the tragedy, despite the fact that the paper that was leaked stated that passengers were informed of an issue 18 minutes before the submersible sank.

The incident, which happened in 2019, is still being investigated as to its root cause.

Authorities are looking into a number of possible scenarios on what may have caused the accident, one of which is whether the submarine was broken.

Reactions to the Leak

The leaked Titan Communications Transcript has caused many reactions from those who are interested in the tragedy. 

Some believe that the information in the transcript confirms their suspicions about the lack of safety regulations. Many call for stricter regulations to guarantee the safety of passengers entering the deep sea.

Others are addressing why the record was spilled and who is answerable for it.

Final thoughts

The recently leaked Titan Communications transcript has generated widespread curiosity about the events that preceded the tragic incident.

The record reveals that passengers were aware of a problem prior to the submersible sinking, but it is still unclear what exactly caused the catastrophe.

To ensure the safety of all deep sea explorers, requests have been made for stricter rules in response to the revelation of this secret paper.

The authorities have not yet commented on the document’s legitimacy. Many people are hopeful that this tragedy will lead to the introduction of improved safety procedures for all passengers aboard submersibles.
