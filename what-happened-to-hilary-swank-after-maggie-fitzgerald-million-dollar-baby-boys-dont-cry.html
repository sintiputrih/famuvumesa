What Happened To Hilary Swank After 'Million Dollar Baby'?

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, February 9, 2024

Hollywood star Hilary Swank rose to fame in the late 90s with one of her most incredible performances — her portrayal of Brandon Teena in the biographical movie BoysDon'tCry. In 2004 the actress portrayed aspiring boxer MaggieFitzgerald in ClintEastwood's sports drama movie MillionDollarBaby. For both of her performances, Hilary won an Academy Award in the category Best Lead Actress.

Today, we're taking a look at just what the actress has been up to since Million Dollar Baby. From focusing on work behind the camera to getting married — keep on scrolling to see just what Hilary Swank has done lately!

10 In 2005 She Was One Of Time Magazine's 100 Most Influential People In The World

A year after Clint Eastwood's Million Dollar Baby came out, the actress was named one of Time magazine's 100 most influential people in the world. Yes, in 2005 Hilary joined other celebs like Quentin Tarantino, Alicia Keys, Kanye West, Bill Gates, Oprah Winfrey, LeBron James, and Barack Obama.

9 In The 2010s Hilary Swank Focused On Producing

One thing many fans may have noticed is that Hilary Swak hasn't appeared in too many projects since 2010. In the past decade, the actress slightly shifted her focus to working behind the scenes.

Over the years Hilary has produced a couple of movies such as Amelia, Conviction, You're Not You, and What They Had.

8 But She Still Starred In Movies Like 'New Year's Eve' And 'The Homesman'

Besides working behind the camera, Hilary Swank has also appeared in a couple of famous movies. Some of her most memorable post-Million Dollar Baby movies include the 2007 romantic drama P. S. I Love You, the 2011 rom-com New Year's Eve, the 2014 Western historical drama The Homesman, and the 2017 heist comedy Logan Lucky.

7 In 2014 She Took A Break From Acting To Help Her Father After A Lung Transplant

In an interview with Health magazine from last year, the actress revealed that she actually took a conscious break from citing in 2014 in order to help her father recover from a lung transplant. Here's what the actress said:

"It was supposed to be a year because it takes a year to see if an organ transplant takes. A lung transplant is the most difficult of all, as it’s an incredibly delicate organ. The plan was to take off a year. I became my dad’s health advocate. One year quickly turned into two and then three. And, thank God, prayers were answered. He is healthy and doing really well five-plus years later."

6 In 2016 Hilary Got Engaged To Financial Advisor Ruben Torres

The Hollywood star started dating financial advisor and former professional tennis player Ruben Torres in 2015.

In March 2016 the couple got engaged however it seems as if they quickly realized things won't work out for them. In June of the same year, the two decided to end their engagement and go their separate ways.

5 In 2018 The Actress Married Entrepreneur Philip Schneider

In November 2016 the actress started dating entrepreneur Philip Schneider and it seems as if they quickly realized they want a future together. After two years of dating, the couple tied the knot on August 18, 2018. Three years later they are still happily married, however, they tend to keep their relationship out of the spotlight.

4 Hilary Starred In The 2019 Sci-Fi Thriller 'I Am Mother'

In 2019 the actress starred in the Australian sci-fi thriller I Am Mother in which she portrayed Woman. Besides Hilary Swank, the movie also stars Clara Rugaard, Rose Byrne, Luke Hawker, Hazel Sandery, Summer Lenton, Maddie Lenton, Tahlia Sturzaker, and Jacob Nolan. Currently, I Am Mother has a 6.7 rating on IMDb.

3 And She Lent Her Voice To Joey Pogo In 'BoJack Horseman'

In the same year, Hilary Swank joined the cast of the popular animated adult sitcom BoJack Horseman which premiered in 2014. In 2019 and 2020 the actress was the voice behind Joey Pogo and she worked alongside a cast that included Will Arnett, Amy Sedaris, Alison Brie, Paul F. Tompkins, and Aaron Paul. Currently, BoJack Horseman has an 8.7 rating on IMDb.

2 In 2020 The Actress Starred In The Netflix Science Drama 'Away'

Last year the actress could also be seen in the Netflix sci-fi drama show Away. In it. Hilary Swank portrayed NASA astronaut Emma Green and she starred alongside Josh Charles, Vivian Wu, Mark Ivanir, Ato Essandoh, Ray Panthaki, and Talitha Bateman. Currently, Away — which was canceled after only one season — has a 6.6 rating on IMDb.

1 Lastly, The Actress Prefers Staying Out Of The Spotlight

And finally, wrapping the list up is the fact that the famous actress seems to prefer staying out of the spotlight. While there's no doubt that Hilary Swank loves her job, it's also noticeable that she prefers living a private life rather than posing for the cameras at red carpet events. Staying out of the spotlight is definitely one of the reasons many might not know what the actress is up to today!
