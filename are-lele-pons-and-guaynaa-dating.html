Are Lele Pons and Guaynaa Dating? &quot;Se Te Nota&quot; Video Has Fans Convinced

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 13, 2024

Following the music video for their new earworm "Se Te Nota," fans are wondering whether Lele Pons is dating collaborator Guaynaa. Keep reading.

Venezuelan-American personality Lele Pons ushered in the age of influencer stars and is the embodiment of social media royalty. The 24-year-old first rose to fame on Vine, becoming the first star on the platform to amass one billion loops, and reaching more than seven billion before the platform shut down. 

Today, Lele has millions of followers on YouTube and Instagram, and naturally, fans want to know everything about her, including her dating life. Recently, there have been rumors that the actress / singer / dancer / model / TV host has been dating Puerto Rican rapper Guaynaa after they appeared in the music video for "Se Te Nota."

Keep reading to learn more about Lele's dating life.

Is Lele Pons dating Guaynaa?

Despite all the swirling rumors that Lele Pons and Guaynaa are a couple, the two don’t actually seem to be dating. It seems that word of a possible romance between the two started when the stars collaborated on Lele’s newest song, “Se Te Nota,” where Guaynaa is featured.

Lele surprised fans back in September when she announced her new single on Instagram and dropped a music video. In the video, fans saw Lele trying to escape from her rapper collaborator as he tries to win her affections. After several rebuffs, Guaynaa’s resilience finally pays off as he and Lele share a kiss. With the release of Lele and Guaynaa’s infectious song and playful music video, fans were quick to speculate about a possible romance between the two. 

Who has Lele Pons dated?

Lele is known to fan the flames when it comes to rumors about her dating life, but be notoriously private about her real love life. Back in 2018, a fan-run Instagram account posted a video of Lele cuddling and kissing social media influencer Ray Diaz, and Ray himself commented saying that he and Lele had already been together for a year.

Prior to that, Lele was rumored to be dating Juanpa Zurita, another social media star. Fans went crazy when the two shared a kiss on the stage of the 2017 MTV Millennial Awards in Mexico, but both Lele and Juanpa denied that they were together.

There were also rumors that Lele was dating King Bach at one point, but neither party confirmed nor denied the rumors. The only dating rumor that Lele has addressed was about her dating fellow YouTube star Twan Kuyper, as they have pretended to be boyfriend and girlfriend before, but are actually just “best friends.”

About her and Twan’s rather intimate looking relationship, Lele said, “I'm not attracted to him, he's not attracted to me, and that's why it's so easy. It’ll never ruin our friendship because we're so unattracted to each other. He's the opposite of what I like in a man.”

In a Q&A with Tinder back in September 2019, Lele opened up about her dating life saying, “I have been through so much. I live in a telenovela … I’ve been the side chick, I’ve been the one that has been cheated on, the one that cheats.” 

Sounds like the internet star has had plenty of real life drama in her love life and doesn’t need to add fake boyfriends to the mix.
