Cameron Diaz Net Worth, Early Life, Career, Career Success, Real Estate and Retirement

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, February 19, 2024

Cameron Diaz is an actress who became the highest-paid actress 2013 in 2013. She has received many accolades including 4 Golden Globe Awards and a BAFTA Award as well. 

Read the full article below to know about the net worth of Cameron Diaz, their early life, career and others.

What is the Net Worth of Cameron Diaz?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the estimated net worth of Cameron Diaz is said to be nearly $140 million. She first gained prominence extensively for appearing in the 1994 film “The Mask.” Cameron Diaz has earned millions from her career as an actress which has contributed majorly to her net worth.

After taking a dig into her net worth, let’s sneak into Cameron Diaz’s early life.

Early Life

Cameron was born in San Diego, California on the 30th of August, 1972. She grew up in Long Beach, California, and went to Long Beach Polytechnic. She was classmates with Snoop Dogg.

Her family wasn’t financially stable and struggled to raise her. When she was 16, she signed with Elite Model Management and soon started her professional modeling. Cameron made her way to several high-profile fashion campaigns during the first five years while living overseas in places like Australia, Japan, and Paris.

Career

Cameron got back home to Southern California at the age of 21. In spite of having no experience in acting, she went for an audition and was booked for the female lead alongside Jim Carrey in “The Mask”. After she got the role, Cameron started taking acting lessons. The film became a hit and grossed over $340 million across the world. She brought immense popularity with this film.

For another few years, she went on to appear in many films including “My Best Friend’s Wedding” (1997). She then reached another scale of success after landing the lead role in the film titled “There’s Something About Mary,” which earned $369 million across the world and brought her first nomination for Golden Globe Award.

Her performance in the 1999 film titled “Being John Malkovich”, was a huge hit. Some of her other notable films include 2000’s “Charlie’s Angels” which grossed $264 million across the world; 2001’s “Vanilla Sky”, which brought her nominations for four honorable Awards, and the 2001 movie “Gangs of New York”.

Cameron also appeared in 2005’s “In Her Shoes”, 2006’s “The Holiday”, 2008’s “What Happens in Vegas”, 2009’s “My Sister’s Keeper”, 2010’s “Knight and Day”, 2011’s “The Green Hornet” and “Bad Teacher”, 2014’s “S*x Tape” and “Annie”, amongst several others. All of these projects played an important role in building Cameron Diaz’s net worth.

She also voiced the character of Princess Fiona in the Shrek franchise.

Real Estate

Diaz owns many opulent estates across the United States, particularly in New York and California. She acquired an abode for $1.34 million in 2001 in Los Angeles above the Sunset Strip. Her primary residence is located in Beverly Hills which she acquired for $9.5 million in 2010. 

She owned a loft in Greenwich Village in New York for several years and sold it for $4 million in 2015. She also owns a $9.5 million condo in the Chelsea neighborhood which was acquired by her in 2013.

Cameron and Joel spent $14.7 million to acquire a new estate in 2020 in Beverly Hills. The abode features 7 bedrooms and 10 bathrooms and sits on 1.7-acres. The house is situated at the top of Beverly Hills in a gated community.

Retirement

In 2014, Diaz retired from her acting career. Since the December 2014 rollout of “Annie.” She hasn’t appeared in a movie or television project.

In 2009, Diaz was given a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
