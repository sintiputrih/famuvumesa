Everything We Know About Lisa Vanderpump's New Show

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, April 4, 2024

In February 2021, E! unveiled the trailer for Lisa Vanderpump's new reality show Oversized With Lisa Vanderpump. The series, which is set to premiere on March 18, 2021, appears to be unlike any of her previous reality show appearances and doesn't involve as much shade throwing. In the preview, Vanderpump shares that she will be holding "an intimate dinner party" for a number of famous faces at her Beverly Hills mansion dubbed Villa Rosa.

The show follows the RHOBH alum as she spoils her famous friends with "an evening of cocktails, games and feasts," according to Variety. The outlet also noted that the themes will include "'Diva Tea,' 'Beverly Hills Comfort Food' and 'Ladies who Brunch.'" Some of the well-known guests include comedians Gabriel Iglesias and Sheryl Underwood, rapper Iggy Azalea, and Vanderpump Rules cast members James Kennedy and Lala Kent.
