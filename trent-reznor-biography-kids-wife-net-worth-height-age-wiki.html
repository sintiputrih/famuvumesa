Trent Reznor Bio (Age)

When they were married, Nancy Lou (née Clark) and Michael Reznor welcomed their son Reznor into the world on May 17, 1965, in New Castle, Pennsylvania, as Michael Trent Reznor. Trent has a sister named Tera. However, the siblings barely grew up together, Reznor’s parents would then come to divorce when he was 6 years old and Reznor would then be raised by his maternal grandparents in Mercer.

The musician, who has his roots in Germany and Ireland, was very active as children. Trent Reznor was a member of the Boys Scout and loved to skateboard and play musical instruments for fun. He started playing the piano from the age of 12 and later on tenor saxophone and tuba. He was part of his high school jazz band.

In high school, Trent Reznor was also very good at acting and was once voted by his classmates as the best in drama. Also in high school, Reznor performed with local rock band Option 30 before graduating in 1983. After graduation, he studied computer engineering at Allegheny College. Cleveland, Ohio in order to pursue a career in music.

Trent Reznor would spend the next two years with several bands, including Urge, The Innocent and Exotic Birds. He began recording his own music at the Right Track studio where he had landed jobs as a janitor and assistant engineer. Playing all the instruments except the drums, Reznor formed the group Nine Inch Nails in 1988 and the following year he released his first album, Jolie machine à la hatred which was a great success propelling him to fame. In 1993, Trent Reznor won his first Grammy Award. Under his band, Reznor released at least 9 studio albums.

wife and children

Trent Reznor is married to a Filipina American singer, songwriter Mariqueen Maandig. The couple united in October 2009 and decided to start a big family. As of this writing, the couple share four children.

Their first child, a son named Lazarus Echo Reznor, was born a year after their marriage on October 10, 2010. Two more sons were born to them on New Year’s Eve 2011 and November 1, 2015 – Balthazar Venn Reznor is the name of the one born in 2011, while the other’s name has since remained hidden from the media. In December 2006, Trent Reznor and his wife welcomed their daughter, who they named Nova Lux Reznor.

After their marriage in 2009, Trent Reznor and his wife joined forces to form a musical group. How to destroy the angels they describe as post-industrial. Prior to the group’s formation, Mariqueen Maandig, wife of Reznor, was the vocalist of West Indian Girl. During her five years with the group from 2004 to 2009, Maandig contributed vocals to two albums released in 2004 and 2007. Maandig was born on April 5, 1981, which made Reznor 16 years older than her. .

Net value

Trent Reznor has achieved huge success with his music with over 20 million records sold worldwide. The musician has an Academy Award to his name that came for his collaboration with Atticus Ross for the 2010 film, The Social Network. Together they scored two more films.

He has amassed a net worth of $70 million from his musical success and other ventures. He is Creative Director of Project Daisy, a music subscription service from Beats Electronics. In 2007, Reznor paid $4.2 million for a Beverly Hills mansion he listed in 2015 for $300,000 more than the purchase price.
