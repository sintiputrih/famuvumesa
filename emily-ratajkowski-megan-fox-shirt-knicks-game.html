Emily Ratajkowski Wears a Megan Fox T-Shirt to a Knicks Game

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, May 15, 2024

Emily Ratajkowski stepped out for a girls’ night in a look that paid tribute to a fellow queer bombshell, Megan Fox.

The model attended Wednesday’s Knicks playoff game at Madison Square Garden in New York City (in which they beat the Miami Heat) with comedian friend Ziwe. The two sat courtside for the game, wearing outfits that showed off their contrasting styles.

EmRata wore a black cropped tee stamped with an image of Fox, looking creepy and bloody, from her cult-classic film Jennifer’s Body. She paired the statement shirt with big blue jeans, which she wore folded and unbuttoned, and white Vans sneakers. She accessorized with some rings and gold earrings, and later layered on a blue-and-orange Knicks x Kith varsity jacket.

Michelle Farsi/ NY Knicks/ MSG Sports

Next to her, Ziwe wore a shimmery ripped Barbie-pink top, matching pants, white Miu Miu sneakers, a pink fuzzy bucket hat, and a pink-and-white beaded necklace with a charm featuring a photo of Oprah Winfrey.

Michelle Farsi/ NY Knicks/ MSG Sports

Ratajkowski’s cheeky tribute to Fox and Jennifer’s Body comes amid her ongoing conversations about body image, female empowerment, dating as a queer person, and the sexualization of the female form. She touches on the topics in her podcast, High Low, as well as in her book, My Body. Fox has also spoken of her sexuality, as well as of her experience being objectified in Hollywood; in 2019 she even told Entertainment Tonight that she had a “genuine psychological breakdown” after being overly sexualized in her role in the 2009 horror-comedy.
