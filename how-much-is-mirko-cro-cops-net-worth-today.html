Who is Mirko Cro Cop?

Mirko Cro Cop, whose real name is Mirko Filipović was born on September 10, 1974 in Vinkovci, SR Croatia, SFR Yugoslavia. He grew up in a family of sports enthusiasts as his father was a wrestler and his brother, a professional basketball.

Mirko ventured into the martial arts space as he was interested and had the love for Taekwondo and Karate during his childhood. He began training at age 13 and soon made the interest in the sports a reality years after.

Mirko Cro Cop attended and graduated from the University of Zagreb with a degree in Kinesiology, which is the scientific study of human movement.

In 1996, Mirko’s career in the combat sports began, when he began training in kickboxing. It wasn’t long until he made a name for himself as one of the top fighters in the sport and won the K-1 World Grand Prix Championship in 2012. He made a switch to the Mixed Martial Arts where he achieved a greater success following an already success kickboxing career.

Through his career, Mirko Cro Cop competed in some of the biggest MMA organizations in the world, including Pride FC, Rizin Fighting Federation and the UFC. He won several accolades including the Pride FC 2006 Open-Weight Grand Prix Championship.
