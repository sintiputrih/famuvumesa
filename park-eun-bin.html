Extraordinary Attorney Woo Actress Park Eun Bin Wiki | Bio

Park Eun Bin (born on September 4, 1992) is a 30 years old actress known for her fantastic performance as Jung Yeo-wool in “The Ghost Detective.” She is also an incredible musician and singer. Bin also worked in movies, including “Death Bell 2: Bloody Camp” and “The Witch: Part 2. The Other One.” Besides movies and shows, Park modeled in around a dozen music videos.

On June 29, 2022, a series titled “Extraordinary Attorney Woo” got released that helped Eun earn worldwide fame. In this show, Bin portrayed the character of a girl named Woo Young Woo, who possesses a high IQ and, during her graduation, tops the law school.

Early Life

On September 4, 1992, Park Eun Bin (age 29) was born in a wealthy family in Songpa-gu, Seoul, South Korea. Park was only 3 when her mother and father decided to introduce her to the art of acting. In 1997 Bin auditioned for a show and instantly got selected to play Choi So-young in a TV serial named “White Nights 3.98.”

After her first acting job, Bin became very successful but did not forget to complete her homework daily. In 2010 following schooling, this Korean actress joined Sogang University to study Psychology and Media Communication.

Career

From 1997 to 2011, Eun worked in two dozen movies and six shows but never got a chance to play a lead role. Finally, in 2012 she played a lead character named Ham Yi-Seul in a romantic show “Operation Proposal” and appeared alongside Yoo Seung Ho. The noteworthy performances of Bin before 2011 were “Gyebaek,” “The Legend,” “The King’s Woman,” “Age of Warriors,” “Seoul 1945,” “Death Bell 2: Bloody Camp,” and “How to Keep My Love.”

In an interview, Bin accepted that she always pays attention to the story before receiving projects and does not care if the role offered to her is major or minor. 2019 to 2020 proved to be the most successful time of Eun’s life as in that period; she worked in hot dramas titled “Do You Like Brahms?” and “Hot Stove League.”

Besides acting, Park also worked extensively as a model. From music videos to fashion shows, Bin ruled Korea’s modeling industry. Music videos in which Eun worked include “Angel’s Prayer,” “Thank You for Loving Me,” “I Still Don’t Know,” “I’ll Be There,” “Cold,” and “Doll.”

Currently, she is at the peak of her career and working actively in the acting and modeling industries. As per trusted sources, Park Eun Bin’s net worth is USD 4.57 Million.

Personal Life

In 2020, after the success of “Do You Like Brahms?” the rumors about the love affair of Bin and Min Jae Kim were all over social media. But those gossips were baseless as neither Eun nor Jae ever commented.

The same thing happened in 2021. After the release of “The King’s Affection,” there was gossiping about her relationship with Rowoon, which was also false news. At present, Bin resides with her family in Seoul, Korea.
