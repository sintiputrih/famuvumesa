25 Stylish Layered Haircuts For Little Girls

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 2, 2024

Layered haircuts look good on every face shape and hair type. Layered cuts can be styled into different variations. This style of haircut has been trending among young girls as it is an easy, cute, and versatile hairstyle.

Layered hair looks are loved by many as it brings volume, and dimensions to the dull hair. Here is the list of 25 stylish layered haircuts that are perfect for your little princess.

1. Long And Sophisticated Layers

A long and sophisticated layer haircut adds texture, dimension, and personality to your little princess’ hairstyle. In this classic and timeless hairstyle, the layers are at the bottom of the length of the hair.

The chic layered hairstyle is perfect and can fit any personality and style. 

2. Inward Curled Layers

Inward-curled layers are a haircut similar to long and sophisticated layers. The only difference is to curl the bottom layers of the hair in the inward direction.

The curl gives a livelier touch to the simple haircut. Style your baby girl’s layered haircut with inward curls for special occasions.

3. Layer Cut For Wavy Hair

Layer cuts for wavy hair help to enhance the natural waves. To get an edgy bouncy look, two-step layers are a suitable option for the wavy hair type.

Wavy layers are the perfect style to make your little ones’ appearance vibrant, and young. If your little munchkins have medium to thick hair density then adding layers to their wavy hair can be flattering. 

4. Straight Hair Layers To The Side

Straight hair layers give the illusion of an extra layer of volume and bounce. The look is achieved by cutting square layers that are short enough to give the top part of the hair some textured volume.

If you are looking to create a soft aesthetic for your baby girl then wispy layers are the best option.

5. Layer Cut With Side Swept Bangs

Layer cut with side-swept bangs adds youthfulness to your little girl’s appearance. The side-swept bangs work well with most hair types, hairstyles, and face shapes.

The layers added to the side-swept bangs help to frame the face and structure the hair. Give your baby girl a dreamy and more stylish layer cut with shaggy side-swept bangs.

6. Short, Face Framing Layered Bob

Face-framing layers are known to add layers around the face and it is styled shorter than the rest of the hair. Short, face-framing layered bob haircut helps to soften and highlight the facial features.

The cool and classy short, face-framing layered bob is considered to be one of the low-maintenance hairstyles. 

7. Layer Cut And Straight Bangs

Straight bangs are known to be the most common haircut among kids as they look adorable with bangs. Layering hair with bangs gives more volume to the hair.

Layer cut with straight bangs might sound simple but the bangs in this style create dimension and add movement to the look. 

8. Short And Wavy Layers

Layering short and wavy hair creates an illusion of the hair being thicker. If your little princess has thick hair then styling the hair in light layers helps to enhance their adorable and angelic appearance.

Layers are known to intensify the appeal of short wavy hairstyles. Short and wavy layers are easier to style and give more free-flowing look.

9. Curly Hair With Short Bangs

Since the early days, there has been a belief that bangs look good only on straight and wavy hair. People get surprised when they learn that curly hair with bangs is also a perfect combination.

Short bangs are the perfect pick for your little girl with curly hair. Short bangs are a sweet way to mix up beautiful curly hair. Short bangs on curly hair must be trimmed a bit longer than the normal short bangs length since they shrink.

10. Wavy Long Hair With Center Part

Long wavy hair with a middle part is a classic look. The long soft waves and the middle partition of hair will make your little girl feel like a princess.

Your little girl will look more classy and sophisticated in wavy long hair with a center part. Hairstyles such as braids, pigtails, waterfall hairstyles, and more are considered to be great hairstyles for  soft and modern look.

11. Shaggy Layers

Shaggy layers are feathered at the top and sides and make the hair full around the crown of the head and thinner around the edges and throughout the ends. This hairstyle gives a modern and sharp look.

This haircut includes tousled layers, face-framing bangs, and choppy ends. Shaggy Layers are a classic yet modern style that is ideal for medium to thick hair types and wavy, straight, and curly hair.

12. Ponytail With Layers

The ponytail is an iconic hairstyle that never goes out of style. There are several variations of ponytails that look adorable on your little girl.

The layered haircut helps the ponytail’s curls stand out on different levels.

The layered haircut has been considered a no for many little girls as the hair sticks out giving it a messy and frizzy appearance. But with the help of hair spray and toothbrush, the frizzy look can be reduced,

Spray hairspray on the toothbrush and brush down the tiny hair.

13. Layered Bob

Layered Bob haircut features cool choppy or fringy edges, beautiful textures, and fun asymmetry. This haircut can be styled in various ways such as voluminous curls, or sleek flat hair. This haircut is known to universal style for every face shape.

A layered bob is perfect for young girls who prefer short hair. There are variations of layered bob that can enhance the facial structure of your baby girl.  The types of layered bob include wispy layered bob, bouncy layered bob, choppy layered bob, layered textured bob, and graduated layer bob.

14. Thin And Layered

Layers are the perfect option for thin hairs as they preserve the hair’s thickness. The long layers also help to add shape to the thin hair.

Short hairstyles are popular for thin-haired people but long layers and blunt ends can provide volume to thin strands. Bluntly cut bangs can be added for framing.

15. Pigtails

Pigtails are a cute hairstyle that gives little girls an adorable look. The middle partition separates two loose ponytails at the side of the head.

A long layered haircut adds style and stops the straight hair from being plain. This hair look can be further styled with different types of bangs such as side swept bangs, straight bangs, short bangs, and more.

16. Straight Bangs With Curly Layers

Layers define the curls and allow them to retain the bounce of the hair. Bangs gives more definition and enhances the style.

Make your little one part of the trend, and style their straight bangs and curly layers as the hairstyle is considered to be the new way to have fun with fringe cuts. Bangs on curly hair is perfect for creating an adorable look.

17. Side Parted Layered Hair

For your little one’s next haircut add feathered ends at the bottom of the long layers and style the haircut with a heavy side part. Stylish side parted layered is ideal for long, slim, and pointed face shapes.

One side of the head must be overweighed by the other in order to create enough volume. In order to enhance the texture scrunch the hair with the help of the fingers and use a little hair spray for an all-day hold.

18. A-Line Bob

A-line bob hairstyle is shorter in the back and gradually lengthens in the front. Depending on the length of the hair the backside of the hair doesn’t have to be short in a dramatic way.

Enhance the facial features of your baby girl with a modern and chic hairstyle. This bob haircut compliments most of the face shapes and hair textures.

19. Short Hair With Layers

Short hair with layers provides more volume, movement, and shape. If your little princess loves short hair then style her look with different variations of short hair with layers.

Layered short hair gives an edgy yet modern and sophisticated look to your baby girl. Style the short hair with different layered styles including:

  • Layered pixie with bangs
  • Wispy layered bob cut
  • Edgy feathered bob
  • Short medium blunt bob

20. Straight Hair With Baby Bangs

If you want to style your little girl’s straight hair with baby bangs then ask the hairdresser to transition the fringe into face-framing layers that add body.

Baby bangs are ideal for face shapes such as round, oval, and heart shapes. The baby bangs soften the features of your baby girl and bring out the natural face shape. Straight hair with short bangs gives a sleek and straight-across look.

21. Short And Angled Layered Bob

Short and angled layered bob gives texture and fullness to tresses. This hairstyle is perfect for your little princess if she has straight hair.

A short and angled layered bob is a cute and classy haircut. The hair look is more suitable for square shaped face.

22. Sharp Short Layers

Short-layer haircut and style has been on the trend for a while. The style has a mix of carefree and edgy look.

23. Short One-Sided Layered Bob

A short one-sided layered bob is a trendy look that gives your young ones an edgy look. The hair on one side of the head is shorter than the other side, the shorter part is cut above the ear.

The short side has more pronounced layers, giving it a choppy and textured appearance.

24. Thick Kinky Curls

A layered haircut for thick kinky curls can provide definition and shape to your little one’s hair. The layers may vary in length and thickness, with shorter layers at the top and longer layers at the bottom. 

The layers will help reduce weight and allow the curls to spring up with more bounce. Stylists may use techniques such as slide-cutting and point-cutting to create soft and blended layers that won’t change the curl patterns.

25. Very Long Layers

Very long layers haircut involves creating layers throughout the length of the long hair to add depth and dimension. The haircut often starts several inches from the end of the hair.

Long layers showcase a soft and sophisticated appearance. According to professional stylist Eloise Chelung, long layers create an optical illusion that frames the face and balances the proportions. U-cut layered and V-cut layered are known to be the best for long hair.
