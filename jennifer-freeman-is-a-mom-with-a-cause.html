JENNIFER FREEMAN IS A MOM WITH A CAUSE

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Actress Jennifer Freeman is a mom with a cause! The My Wife and Kids star has partnered with Step Up Women’s Network to help women and girls advance.

Nationally recognized for its charitable efforts, the Step Up Women’s Network is “dedicated to connecting and advancing women and girls. By bridging communities of professional women and underserved teen girls through mentorship, networking and advancement, we ensure women and girls have the opportunities they need to create a better future.”

Freeman is the proud mother of toddler Isabella Amour Watson (b. 2009). Isabella is Jennifer and basketball player Earl Watson’s only child together.
