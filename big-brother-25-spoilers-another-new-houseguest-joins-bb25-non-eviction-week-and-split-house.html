Big Brother 25 Spoilers: Another New Houseguest Joins BB25 Non-Eviction Week and Split House

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 20, 2024

Big Brother 25 (BB25) spoilers tease that CBS is gearing up to flip the house once again. The season is only a few days in, but it’s shaping up to be a twisted season.

The buzz on social media reveals that the nominations and Power of Veto competition for Week One could be all for nothing.

Supposedly, there will not be an eviction this week, and Reilly Smedley, current Head of Household, will be able to compete in the next HOH comp on Thursday, August 10.

Big Brother 25 spoilers say that at least one more houseguest will join the cast. There is some chatter online that there could be three more to join the game.

That would make sense as Julie Chen Moonves teased that Frankie Grande, Danelle Reyes, and Brittany Hanyes could return to right the wrongs from their seasons.

As for how the game will be played, the Twitter leaker says that the house could be split in half, with two games being played simultaneously. That could be an exciting twist.

There is some speculation that the house could be divided into coaches, with past Big Brother players acting as BB coaches for the newbies.

It’s hard to imagine Big Brother crowning Reilly as Head of Household and commencing a Power of Veto comp to cancel the eviction for the week. However, anything is possible in the Big Brother multiverse.

Big Brother 25 livefeed viewers expressed their opinion about this possible twist. They are not happy with the idea.

The whole week would be for nothing. No one liked the idea of bringing three more houseguests into the game during Week Two.
