Camila Cabello Opens the Show with Dont Go Yet at Billboard Latin Music Awards 2021 (Video)

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, January 12, 2024

Camila Cabello gave an energetic performance to kick off the 2021 Billboard Latin Music Awards!

The 24-year-old singer opened the show with her performance on Thursday night (September 23) at the Watsco Center in Coral Gables, Fla.

Camila performed her hit new single “Don’t Go Yet.” The show aired on Hispanic entertainment cable channel Universo and also featured performances from Marc Anthony, Juanes, Prince Royce, and more.

Earlier in the night, Camila looked incredible while wearing a short red dress on the red carpet.
