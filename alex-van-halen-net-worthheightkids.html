Alex Van Halen Net Worth, Height, Kids, Age &amp; More

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 18, 2024

Alex Van Halen, renowned for his mastery behind the drum kit, has amassed a significant net worth of $95 million.

As a co-founder and the rhythmic backbone of the iconic rock band Van Halen, his contributions to the music industry have not only solidified his status as a legendary drummer but have also translated into substantial financial success.

Who Is Alex Van Halen?

Alex Van Halen is a famous American musician and a co-founder of the rock band Van Halen.

He was born in Amsterdam, Netherlands, on May 8, 1953, and moved to Pasadena, California, with his family when he was nine years old.

He started his musical career as a guitarist, but switched to drums after his younger brother Eddie showed more talent on the guitar.

Together, they formed several bands before settling on Van Halen in 1974, with David Lee Roth as the lead singer and Michael Anthony as the bassist.

Van Halen became one of the most successful and influential rock bands of all time, selling over 80 million albums worldwide and earning induction into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2007.

Alex Van Halen was the only constant member of the band besides his brother Eddie, who died in 2020.

Alex Van Halen is known for his powerful and energetic drumming style, which incorporates double bass pedals, syncopated rhythms, and complex fills.

He has also composed and arranged some of the band’s songs, such as “Hot for Teacher” and “Everybody Wants Some!!”.

Besides his musical talent, Alex Van Halen also has a side job as an ordained minister.

He officiated the wedding of his brother Eddie Van Halen and Janie Liszewski in 2009.

He has been playing drums since he was a child and has influenced many other musicians with his style and technique.

Alex Van Halen Kids

Alex Van Halen, the co-founder and drummer of the rock band Van Halen, has two sons from different marriages.

The first son, Aric Van Halen, was born in 1986 to Alex’s first wife, Valeri Kendall.

Aric Van Halen is a professional athlete who competes in the 3,000 meter steeplechase.

He has represented the United States at international events and has a personal best of 8:32.92.

Aric Van Halen inherited his father’s musical talent and plays drums as well.

The second son, Malcolm Van Halen, was born in 2008 to Alex’s current wife, Stine Schyberg.

Malcolm Van Halen is the youngest member of the Van Halen family and has a close bond with his father and uncle, Eddie Van Halen.

He also shows interest in music and drums, and often accompanies his father to concerts and rehearsals.

Malcolm Van Halen is named after Malcolm Young, the rhythm guitarist of AC/DC, who was one of Alex’s favorite musicians.
