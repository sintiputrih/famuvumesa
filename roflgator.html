Roflgator

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, September 12, 2024

Roflgator, also known as Robert “Rob” Malecki, is a full-time Twitch streamer who was recently banned from the streaming platforms with his fellow collaborator ‘Sodapoppin’ for violating specific Twitch policy.

Roflgator is very famous for his professional E-sports and coaching background and is often seen on streaming platforms like Twitch and YouTube. His YouTube channel uploads weekly videos starring highlights from his Twitch stream or events recorded off-stream and featuring his gameplay.
