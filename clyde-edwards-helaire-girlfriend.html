Meet Clyde Edwards-Helaires girlfriend, JoJo OConner

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Since 2020, Clyde Edwards-Helaire has been enjoying his term as the Kansas City Chiefs of the NFL. He was named as the starting running back for the team. Additionally, He was also the youngest player to run for at least 130 yards in his first NFL game.

Fans wishing to learn about Clyde Edwards-Helaire’s girlfriend and their relationship will get all the answers in this article.

Who is Edwards-Helaire’s girlfriend?

Clyde Edwards-Helaire’s girlfriend is JoJo O’Conner. She is the biggest supporter of Cylde and she has visited Arrowhead Stadium multiple times on and off to either cheer or accompany the player.

The duo shares an interest in traveling and are often seen traveling together, as shared by Clyde on his Instagram post.

Jojo O’Conner’s Early Life, and Career

Jojo O’Conner was born on December 18, 1998. There is no information available on her parents or early life at the moment. However, it is known that she is a graduate of Xavier University of Louisiana. She completed her study in Biology with minors in Chemistry and Women’s Studies in May 2021.

Later, Jojo joined STEM NOLA’s student crew in 2019. She contributes to giving opportunities for New Orleans youth in science and technology.

How did Clyde Edwards-Helaire and Jojo O’Conner meet?

Edwards-Helaire and O’Conner started dating during their college time. However, they were first seen together in July 2022. Later, almost after a year, in June 2023, Edwards-Helaire shocked her with a proposal while they were visiting their new house.

In October, the pair confirmed their wedding plans on Instagram. The duo are also proud dog parents and are often seen sharing stories with the pet.

Jojo O’Conner’s Social Media Presence

Jojo O’Conner has an Instagram account with 2,157 followers. Her Instagram id is teniajojo. She has shared some of her pictures from her university time on her profile. She has also shared a few posts from Clyde Edwards-Helaire’s matches.

Additionally, JoJo also has an official TikTok account. She uses it to show her support for her partner and his team. She has recorded her visits to Arrowhead Stadium on her platform, including the AFC championship triumph in 2023.

Conclusion

Clyde Edwards-Helaire and Jojo O’Conner look cute together. If they have any wedding plans shortly, it will be exciting to see the pair tie the knot.
