GMAs Robin Roberts finally reveals wedding date as she goes missing from morning show in preparat

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 25, 2024

GOOD Morning America's Robin Roberts has finally revealed her wedding date with fiancée Amber Laign.

The TV personality bid farewell to her co-hosts earlier this week as she steps away from the show to prepare to walk down the aisle.

Robin took to X, formerly known as Twitter, to share a sweet message with fans that included a notable detail about her upcoming wedding.

"T-Minus 1 week until the big day… of course still have time to say Rabbit Rabbit," the GMA star wrote, a term she uses on the first day of every new month.

Robin added: "Wishing all blessings this month and always."

This would mean Robin's big day is next Friday, and fans were quick to share in her excitement in the comments section.

"So excited for you both! Sending lots of blessings your way for a beautiful wedding day!" one person wrote.

Another shared: "So happy for you," while a third commented, "Countdown!"

Even fellow GMA personality Gio Benitez responded: "The countdown is officially ON."

Gio was on hand for Robin's last day on the set before she departed to become a married woman.

"Laugh together all the time, enjoy that moment together, be present together, as you always are, you know that," Gio told Robin as words of encouragement before her special day.

When her fellow co-hosts asked Robin how she was feeling about the important event, the 62-year-old proclaimed: "Abundantly blessed."

WEDDING CRAZE

Days earlier, Robin and her soon-to-be wife Amber, 48, were taken aback when meeting up with their wedding event planner, JoAnn Gregoli, to take a look at some of their decorations.

The duo visited JoAnn at Jen Gould Event Design's brick-and-mortar location in New York for GMA's Road to Ring segment.

JoAnn first showed the loving couple a green hedge garden wall that had a monogram of the first initial in Robin and Amber's name.

They were also shown a preview of "what the overall room [used for their wedding reception] would look like."

Placed along the tables were bouquets of flowers filled with pastel white, pink, and orange roses, in addition to pink tulips.

JoAnna also revealed champagne flutes with gold trim, as well as gold-colored candles, and white place settings arranged on the tables.

Robin and Amber also appeared to go wedding cake shopping in July.

The duo met in 2005 after being set up on a blind date.

SPECIAL TIMES

Robin first announced her engagement earlier this year during a New Year's segment on the power ABC morning show.

While chatting with author Gabby Bernstein, Robin was asked to share what she’s saying “yes” to in 2023.

At the time, she revealed that she's engaged, sharing: “OK, I’m saying yes to marriage."
