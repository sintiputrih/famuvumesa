Brooklyn Beckham Shares Photo of Chloe Grace Moretz After Relationship Confirmed

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, April 29, 2024

ICYMI: Chloë Grace Moretz and Brooklyn Beckham are officially dating. That's right, after considerable speculation and plenty of adorable Instagrams, the actress finally confirmed that she and eldest Beckham are in a relationship on Watch What Happens Live earlier this week.

With their relationship status now official, Beckham did what he does best—he shared a photo of them on Instagram. In the pic, Beckham made a digital collage of the duo, cropping images of them which he placed over a pink background of floating hearts. A bit strange, but also kind of adorable, right? He simply captioned the image with a heart emoji.

With Beckham's proclivity for sharing adorable 'grams, we expect he'll be sharing a lot more now that things are official. We can't wait. Keep reading to see their cutest Instagrams so far.
