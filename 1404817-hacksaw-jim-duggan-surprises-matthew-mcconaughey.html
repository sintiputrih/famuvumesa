Hacksaw Jim Duggan Surprises Matthew McConaughey On WWEs The Bump

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, September 7, 2024

HOOOOOOOOO!

WWE Hall of Famer Hacksaw Jim Duggan gave Hollywood star Matthew McConaughey a pleasant surprise. Duggan showed up on WWE’s The Bump and conversed with the Interstellar star.

Matthew McConaughey was a special guest on this week’s new episode of WWE’s The Bump. He was there to promote his upcoming children’s book. Kayla Braxton was the one to interview the Hollywood star. During the interview, Braxton asked about McConaughey’s interest in WWE. To which he revealed that Hacksaw Jim Duggan is one of his favorite wrestlers of all time.

As the Hollywood star mentioned Hacksaw Jim Duggan’s weapon of choice, Braxton also showed the iconic weapon and even offered to send it to McConaughey’s house.

Here Comes Chaos

“And I used to go to a lot of matches when I was younger. I used to go to Hirsch Coliseum in Shreveport, Louisiana… Well, Hacksaw Jim Duggan was my early favorite. Come out with a two-by-four. How, and there it is. Come on, come on… Oh please! That’s it, that’s the two by four. The red, white, blue two-by-four. You better back up. Lock the doors. Here comes some chaos.

“Who else did I see when we were back there? Everyone had a different style, and I love how to succeed in it, you gotta be athletic, you gotta be somewhat intimidating. You gotta sometimes, I love it when somebody has whatever their secret is. Whether it be the claw or whatever that is, that is their signature move,” Matthew McConaughey said.

As the two were talking, the WWE Hall of Famer popped up on camera. Both the actor and wrestler complimented each other for their work. The conversation came to an end with both McConaughey and Hacksaw Jim Duggan yelling the veteran’s signature ‘Ho’.
