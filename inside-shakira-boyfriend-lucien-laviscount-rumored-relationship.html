Everything We Know About Shakiras Rumored And Secretive Relationship With Lucien Laviscount

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, February 16, 2024

Highlights

  • Shakira keeps her love life private, but recent photos with Lucien Laviscount sparked dating rumors.
  • Sources suggest that their potential relationship is likely casual, as Shakira isn't ready for anything serious.
  • Shakira admits she used to feel emotionally dependent on men, but now values being independent.

From "Underneath Your Clothes" to "Hips Don't Lie," Shakira is famous for music ranging from emotional ballads to jams just begging for a dance party. But while she's a passionate and enthusiastic performer, she's more tight-lipped about her love life. While Shakira talked about her split from Gerard Piqué after their 12-year relationship ended badly, she hasn't said much about her other partners.

In March 2024, Shakira was linked to Emily In Paris star Lucien Laviscount, which would be her first romance since her terrible breakup. In this article, we'll take a look at the dating rumors about Shakira and Laviscount, along with her past relationships.

Inside Shakira's Rumored Relationship With Lucien Laviscount

At the end of March 2024, Shakira and Lucien Laviscount went out for dinner in New York City, which led to fans wondering if they were dating. According to Entertainment Tonight, the singer performed in Times Square and then the two stars had a meal at Carbone, an Italian restaurant in Greenwich Village. Shakira and Laviscount looked happy in the photos that were taken of them.

If Shakira and Lewis Laviscount are dating, they are being very secretive about the possible relationship. Fans should know that neither star has commented on the dating rumors. Laviscount appeared in the music video for Shakira's song "Puntería." It's unclear how much time they have spent together since then. If they hung out more than once, then they did so privately and no paparazzi snapped photos.

The stars pretended to be romantically involved in the music video and both looked great. Besides their dinner in New York City, this is another reason why people wonder if they developed romantic feelings for each other.

According to Entertainment Tonight, Lucien Laviscount was at Shakira's concert. Since they seem to have met when he starred in her music video, it's possible that they are just friends who like spending time together and that he wanted to support her NYC performance. Although they were of course aware that photos were taken of them and that people were talking about them, they didn't seem to want to address the rumors publicly and wanted to keep their actual connection a secret.

If the two are dating, it seems unlikely that the relationship is serious. A source told Us Weekly at the end of March 2024 that Shakira would be okay with dating someone if it was "casual.". The source said she "isn't ready for a relationship" and added:

“Shakira has a new light around her that she hasn’t had since before Piqué."

Based on what this source said, it's possible that if Shakira and Lucien Laviscount are dating, it is "casual" and the singer is totally fine with that. Since she shared that she loved Gerard Piqué in several interviews since the beginning of their relationship, she might say more about Laviscount in the future if they are officially together. But as of the time of this writing, she is keeping her dating life a secret.

According to various sources, Shakira's former nanny, Lili Melgar, played a key role in letting the artist know about Gerard Piqué's infidelity.

While at the time of this writing, Shakira hasn't talked about Lucien Laviscount and fans can only speculate about their potential connection, the singer talked about her love life in an interview for the Mexican TV channel Televisa.

She explained that in the past, she thought that she needed to be in a relationship. She said:

“I also fell for this fable that a woman needs a man, because I’ve also always been quite emotionally dependent on men, I have to admit. I’ve been in love with love and I think now, I’ve managed to understand this story from another perspective and understand that I myself am enough.”

While time will tell if Shakira and Lucien Laviscount are dating, it's interesting to note that Shakira and Gerard Piqué began dating after meeting because he appeared in her 2010 music video for "Waka Waka (This Time for Africa)." This at least suggests that she is open to dating someone whom she meets because of her music career.

Who Has Shakira Dated Besides Gerard Piqué?

While the singer's longest relationship was with Gerard Piqué, Shakira has dated several other people. According to Life & Style Magazine, her two most notable relationships were withAntonio de la Rúa and Osvaldo Rios. She and de La Rua dated from 2000 to 2010 and she wrote on her website it was "a mutual decision to take some time apart." She and Rios were a couple for eight months in 1997.

Of course, like many other celebrities, Shakira is often the subject of dating rumors. While she has never confirmed any of them, people have always talked about who she could potentially be seeing.
