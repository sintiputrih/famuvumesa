Veronica Castro Biography, Age, Height, Net Worth &amp; Other Facts

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

Many Mexican actors and actresses have contributed to the success of the Mexican film industry. One of them is Veronica Castro. Apart from being an actress, Castro is a former model and she has gained huge admiration as a singer, producer and presenter.

Veronica Castro Biography (Age)

Born on October 19, 1952, Veronica Judith SaenzCastro Alba has been an active actress since 1964. She was born in Mexico City, DF Veronica has long been active as a singer and actress. In 1973 she recorded her first album, Veronica Castro and following this she released three singles from her next album Sensaciones. In 1974, she recorded another title, “Aprendi A Llorar”. It was just the beginning of a musical career that saw the creation of many beautiful songs.

As an actress, she has appeared in many films and television works. She rose to fame with the telenovela Los rico tambien lloran where she played a poor orphan. As the single-camera sitcom garnered huge attention from viewers around the world, Veronica’s popularity touched the skies. Two years later, she played the lead role in El derecho de nacer, another popular telenovela. She also started recording her music in different languages ​​as her fame skyrocketed.

Veronica Castro performed a Latin version of “We Are The World” in 1986. She then released one of her most ambitious albums to date, Simplemente Todo. As most of her songs were accompanied by videos, something quite rare in songs at the time, she became one of the pioneers of the MTV age in Latin America. In 1987, her career was revived by the role of the main character in Rosa Salvage. The theme song for this telenovela, also named after the movie, became one of its biggest hits. During the 1990s, Castro recorded many singles and participated in different television shows. Till date, she still hosts a variety of shows. Most recently, she was cast as Virginia de la Mora in the Netflix comedy-drama series, The House of Flowers. The web TV series created by Manolo Caro premiered sometime in August 2018.

His net worth

For an artist of his caliber, it’s only fitting that many people are interested in the extent of the wealth Castro has made from his pursuits in the entertainment industry. Well, Veronica Castro has truly amassed a lot of wealth through her career. In January 2018, his net worth was around $30 million.
