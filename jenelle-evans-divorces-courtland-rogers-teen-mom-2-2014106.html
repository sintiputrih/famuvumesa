Jenelle Evans Officially Divorces Courtland Rogers: Teen Mom 2 Split

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, May 19, 2024

Just in time for baby Kaiser’s arrival! More than a year since her December 2012 wedding to Courtland Rogers, Teen Mom 2 star Jenelle Evans officially divorced her ex-hubby on Tuesday, June 10. 

The pregnant reality star officially called it quits from Rogers in April 2013, but according to North Carolina law, there had to be a year of legal separation before the court will grant a divorce. 

"Jenelle was granted the divorce today. She seemed very happy to be moving on,” Evans’ longtime lawyer Dustin Sullivan tells Us Weekly. 

Now the MTV starlet is focusing on the impending arrival of her second son, who she plans to name Kaiser. She is currently dating baby daddy Nathan Griffith, and now that she is legally divorced, there might be even further commitment in the couple’s future. 

"We've been talking about a summer wedding, possibly," the TV star revealed during Teen Mom 2's aftershow in April 2014. "We're not engaged yet or anything, but soon we might be. It's just we don't want to make the wrong decision and give the image out that 'Jenelle's still married and now she's engaged!'"

Evans took to Twitter on June 10 to share her excitement over her finalized divorce.

“Thanks everyone for the positive vibes! I'm very happy and feel so free now! Lol,” she tweeted after her court appearance.
