Taron Egerton also known as Taron David Egerton is a Welsh actor and model. As of 2022, Taron Egerton’s net worth is $6 million. He found prominence in his career when he was cast in Matthew Vaughn’s Kingsman: The Secret Service back in 2012.

He reprised the role of Gary “Eggsy” Unwin in the 2017 sequel of the film, Kingsman: The Golden Circle. Taron got the pivotal role of his career when he was cast in Dexter Fletcher’s musical biopic, Rocketman where he portrayed the role of Elton John.

Taron has received the honorary Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Motion Picture Musical or Comedy and also received numerous other accolades including the BAFTA nomination. Taron has been part of many other recognizable films and television shows such as Billionaire Boys Club (2018), The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance (2019), Watership Down (2018), Eddie the Eagle (2016), Legend (2015), Lewis (2013), etc. He is going to be seen in the autobiographical television series, In with the Devil and in Jon S. Baird’s biographical drama film, Tetris.

Born on 10 November 1989, Taron Egerton’s age is 33 Years Old as of 2022. His birthplace is Birkenhead, Merseyside, United Kingdom.
