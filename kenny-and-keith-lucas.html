Kenny and Keith Lucas Social Network

Timeline

The Lucas Brothers released their first comedy special, On Drugs, on Netflix in April 2017. Paste ranked it as the 9th best comedy special of 2017.

They made their late-night debut on Late Night With Jimmy Fallon in 2012. The duo created the animated series Lucas Bros. Moving Co.. They starred in truTV's series Friends of the People. They appeared in the feature film 22 Jump Street (2014). They appeared in episodes of the Fox TV series The Grinder in 2016 and have played themselves in the Netflix series Lady Dynamite.

The brothers both graduated with honors from the College of New Jersey in 2007 with degrees in philosophy. Kenny attended New York University School of Law and Keith attended Duke University School of Law until the middle of their third year of law school, when they dropped out to pursue stand-up comedy full-time.

Kenny Lucas and Keith Lucas (born September 13, 1985), collectively referred to as The Lucas Brothers, are American identical twin comedians, actors, writers, and producers.
