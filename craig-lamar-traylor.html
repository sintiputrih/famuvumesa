Craig Lamar Traylor Career

Craig Lamar Traylor is an American actor renowned for portraying Stevie Kenarban across 56 episodes on the hit Fox sitcom “Malcolm in the Middle.” He also played a supporting role in the movie “Get a Clue.” Traylor made his acting debut in a 1996 “ER” episode titled “Dead of Winter,” where he stepped into the shoes of Michael. 

In 2011, he further showcased his talents by appearing in the direct-to-DVD release “Dance Fu.” Born in San Bernardino County, California, Traylor is not only a professional actor but also an alumnus of Rancho Cucamonga High School. Further, he is known for his participation in the film “Matilda,” which featured Mara Wilson as the lead character.
