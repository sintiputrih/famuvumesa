Josh Hutcherson on Vanessa Hudgens: &quot;We Love Being Together&quot;

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

Are they or aren't they? Even Josh Hutcherson seems puzzled about the nature of his relationship with Vanessa Hudgens, his costar in Journey 2: The Mysterious Island.

"Oh, boy. I don't know if 'dating' is the right word," he tells the October issue of Seventeen. "She's awesome. We love being together."

"When I first met her, we really hit it off," Hutcherson adds of the actress, 22. "We could be goofy and silly and not worry about anything except having fun. I adore her."
