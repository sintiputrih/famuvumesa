Tori Spellings Ex Dean McDermott Shows Off Black Eye After He Moves In With Girlfriend

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, January 17, 2024

Highlights

  • Dean showed off his black eye after being injured during a hockey game - fans express concern over his well-being.
  • Dean has moved in with his new girlfriend, Lily Calo, after announcing his divorce from Tori Spelling last fall.
  • Tori has confirmed that she and Dean are moving forward with their divorce - both are ready to move on with their lives.

As Tori Spelling and Dean McDermott continue to navigate their divorce, Dean just celebrated a milestone in his new relationship – they’re already moved in. However, fans are growing concerned after Dean recently showed off a black eye.

Dean, 57, took to Instagram to flaunt his injury, explaining it happened after her got injured during a recreational game of hockey.

Dean Says He Got Injured During A Hockey Game

"Got clipped with a high stick today," Dean wrote alongside an image of his bruised eye. "Being sober, I appreciate my beloved game of hockey even more.

"Ken Dryden said, 'hockey doesn’t build character, it reveals it.' I sure have met a lot of characters in my 50 years of playing,” he continued. "Hockey is my Church!! Best Game EVER!!!!"

While some of the comments on Dean’s candid post were encouraging, others warned the father-of-six to be careful. "Just stay sober, Dean. No matter what!" one fan begged. You are a great guy and can do great things …. When you are clean and sober,” one person wrote, while others said, "Ouch" and "Be Careful."

Tori Didn’t Care That Dean Moved In With His New Girlfriend

It was first reported in February that Dean moved in with his girlfriend, Lily Calo, after they got together last fall. Dean announced his divorce from Tori in a note shared to his socials, which he quickly deleted, briefly prompting speculation of a reconciliation. However, Tori has since confirmed they’re moving forward with the divorce, and Dean’s new relationship status is further proof they’ve moved on from one another.

"Dean’s saying he and Lily are in love and ready for the next step," a source said, Ok Magazine! reports.

"She’s been amazingly supportive of his journey to sobriety, and they’re spending so much time together, it just made sense for him to move in," they continued, adding that Dean’s issues with substance abuse as well as infidelity contributed to his and Tori’s decision to end their marriage. He spent several months in a sober living facility following their break-up and started his relationship with Lily a few months later.

"It was like, whatever makes you happy. Tori has moved on too," the source said, noting Tori didn’t have a problem when she learned her estranged husband was taking the next step with his girlfriend.

Tori and Dean tied the know in May 2006 in a ceremony in Fiji, after meeting on the set of the TV movie Mind Over Murder in 2005. At the time, both Spelling and McDermott were married to other people. Tori Spelling was wed to actor-writer Charlie Shanian from 2004 to 2006, while Dean McDermott was married to actress Mary Jo Eustace from 1993 to 2006, with whom he shares a son named Jack, born in 1998.

During their relationship, Tori and Dean appeared together on several reality shows, notably Tori & Dean: Inn Love (2007-2012) and Tori & Dean: Home Sweet Hollywood (2008-2012). They share five children: Liam (born 2007), Stella (born 2008), Hattie (born 2011), Finn (born 2012), and Beau (born 2017). The former couple’s divorce has yet to be finalized.
