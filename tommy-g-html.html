Tommy G - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 1, 2024

YouTube content creator and personality who is best known for his self-titled channel. He publishes interviews with strangers as well as documenting new experiences. He has more than 1.2 million subscribers on the platform. He has also been an aspiring rapper. His "Kia Boys Documentary (A Story of Teenage Car Theft)" video went viral with more than 3 million views.

Before Fame

He made his YouTube debut in 2015 with the video, Tommy G - Banger (UW Party Anthem) [4K]." 

Trivia

He spent a night living on the streets of a dangerous US city. He posted a video about walking through one of the most dangerous neighborhoods in Philadelphia, which received over 500,000 views in less than a week.

Family Life

He and his significant other married in October 2022. They have a pet dog named Frank. 

Associated With

He made a freestyle rap about Joe Exotic of Tiger King fame.
