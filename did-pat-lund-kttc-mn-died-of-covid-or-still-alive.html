Did Pat Lund KTTC MN Died Of Covid Or Still Alive? What To Know About The Journalist - Cause of Deat

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

Pat Lund KTTC MN did not die of Covid. As per the reports his death was assumed to be due to cancer, however, his family had not provided any information or other reliable source spoke about it.

There was a rumor of his death on January 16, 2022. However, his wife Jamie shared his death news posting it on her Facebook page. She wrote, “We lost our hero. I’m sharing this because, by now, our closest friends and family know.”

It is with heavy hearts that we share that our friend and former KTTC sports director, Pat Lund has passed away after a long battle with an illness. Pat was a tremendous supporter of student athletes in SE MN.
