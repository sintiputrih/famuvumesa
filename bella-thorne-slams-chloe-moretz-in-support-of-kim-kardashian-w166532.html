Bella Thorne Slams Chloe Moretz in Support of Kim Kardashian

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, April 25, 2024

Tell us how you really feel! On Tuesday, March 8, Bella Thorne took to social media to weigh in on the current feud between Kim Kardashian and Chloë Grace Moretz. The former Disney star made it quite clear that she is Team Kardashian in the dispute over the reality superstar’s recent nude selfie.

When Thorne, 18, hosted an #askbella Q&A on Twitter for International Women’s Day, she was asked for her opinion on what might be the most unexpected celeb feud of 2016.

“@bellathorne what do you think of kim k ‘s nude pics? #askbella#internationalwomensday,” one fan inquired.

.@chloemoretz_ @KimKardashian I think every woman should be allowed to make their own choices with their OWN bodies soo..

— bella thorne (@bellathorne) March 8, 2016

“.@chloemoretz_ @KimKardashian I think every woman should be allowed to make their own choices with their OWN bodies soo…,” the Scream star responded before revealing where her allegiance lies in the great Moretz vs. Kardashian debate: “.@chloemoretz_ @KimKardashian -and it’s not our place to tell them what they can and can’t do with their body.. #internationalwomensday.”

.@chloemoretz_ @KimKardashian -and it’s not our place to tell them what they can and can’t do with their body ..#internationalwomensday

— bella thorne (@bellathorne) March 8, 2016

Thorne is a longtime friend of the Kardashian-Jenner clan. The “Jersey” singer, who is closest with fellow teenager Kylie Jenner, has never been shy about expressing her love for the famous family.

“I love Kylie. She’s supersweet,” Thorne told HuffPost Live in December 2015. “Kylie and I don’t really get to hang out all that much. We used to hang out more often when we were both a little bit younger. She’s so sweet.… We have lots of mutual friends. I sat next to her at Fashion Week and we were talking. She’s awesome, and we said we would catch up and stuff, and she’s really great.”

As Us Weekly previously reported, Kardashian, 35, lashed out at Moretz, 19, in a heated tweet after the Carrie actress took aim at the E! personality for posting a naked picture of herself online on Monday, March 7.

“@KimKardashian I truly hope you realize how important setting goals are for young women, teaching them we have so much more to offer than just our bodies,” the 30 Rock alum tweeted on Monday, March 7, hours after Kardashian shared her provocative picture.

“let’s all welcome @ChloeGMoretz to twitter, since no one knows who she is. your nylon cover is cute boo,” the mom of two sniped.

Bette Midler shared sentiments similar to Moretz’s earlier the same day. “Kim Kardashian tweeted a nude selfie today,” the Hollywood vet, 70, wrote. “If Kim wants us to see a part of her we’ve never seen, she’s gonna have to swallow the camera.” (Kardashian previously made an infamous sex tape with ex Ray J in 2003, and posed completely nude for Paper magazine last year.)

Mrs. Kanye West took a page out of her husband’s book and shot back at Midler, saying: “Hey @BetteMidler I know it’s past your bedtime but if you’re still up and reading this send nudes #justkidding.”

In a follow-up post, Kardashian went from cheeky to cutting: “hey @BetteMidler I really didn’t want to bring up how you sent me a gift awhile back trying to be a fake friend then come at me #dejavu.”

Regardless of the backlash, Kardashian let her 41.4 million followers know she remains unbothered. She uploaded yet another naked snapshot and captioned it “#liberated.”
