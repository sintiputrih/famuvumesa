How do you get a monolith dragon?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, May 20, 2024

Answer

All six Monolith Dragons may be produced in the Breeding Cave/Epic Breeding Island by using any two Monolith Dragons, in any sequence, at the Breeding Cave or Epic Breeding Island.

What is the best way to breed a tulip dragon?

Breeding. The Tulip Dragon may be created in the Breeding Cave/Epic Breeding Island by using a Sixth Monolith Dragon and a Flower Dragon, in any sequence, to produce a Sixth Monolith Dragon and a Flower Dragon.

What is the best way to breed a concealed bolt dragon?

Breeding. The Hidden Bolt Dragon may be created in the Breeding Cave/Epic Breeding Island by using any two dragons, in any sequence, that possess the Dark and Lightning components, then breeding them together.

What is the best way to breed a tansy dragon?

Breeding. In order to breed the Tansy Dragon in the Breeding Cave/Epic Breeding Island, you must first breed a Fourth Monolith Dragon and then a Cactus Dragon in any sequence.

What is the total number of breeding caves in Dragonvale?

Every player will begin with one Runic Breeding Cave, and they will be given a bonus Runestone that will allow them to activate a second cave during the instructional phase.
