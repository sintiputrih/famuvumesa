Air Jordan 1 High Zoom CMFT 2 &quot;Blackened Blue&quot; Revealed

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 12, 2024

Over the years, Jumpman has come through with a plethora of variations to the Air Jordan 1. One of these offerings is none other than the Air Jordan 1 High Zoom CMFT. Overall, this is a shoe that is characterized by the Zoom air unit in the midsole. So far, it has proven to be a very popular offering, and fans have been looking forward to more. In fact, Jordan Brand has subsequently come out with the Air Jordan 1 High Zoom CMFT 2. Sure, it is quite the mouthful, however, fans are still copping it.

Whenever Jumpman comes out with a new silhouette, you can be sure that a ton of colorways are going to follow suit. When it comes to this new offering, that is most definitely the case. Over the past few months, this shoe has been hit with a ton of great teasers. For the most part, fans are loving what they're seeing, and as a result, Jordan Brand will not slow down. With this latest color scheme, Jumpman has another hit on its hands.

A New Air Jordan 1 High Zoom CMFT

Image via Nike

This color scheme is actually going to be called "Blackened Blue" and it is very easy to see why. Firstly, the shoe actually opens up with some sail coloring on the toe box and the side panels. Secondly, the overlays here are black, except for on the top cuff and the midfoot. The colors that are found in these particular places are just blue. Overall, it makes for a very nice look that fans can definitely appreciate and get behind.

At this point in time, the shoe does not have an official release date. However, it should be dropping very soon for a price of $150 USD. If you are a fan of these, definitely consider scooping them up. As always, let us know what you think of these shoes, down in the comments section below.
