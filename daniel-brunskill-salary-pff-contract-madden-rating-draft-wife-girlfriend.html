Daniel Brunskill is an American football guard for the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League.

Daniel Zachary Brunskill was born on January 27, 1994, in San Diego, California, the United States to parents, Susan Brunskill and Michael Brunskill.

Daniel Brunskill played as a tight end for Valley Center High School in Valley Center, California where received an award for all-league in the lineman category under legendary mentor Coach Rob Gilster.

Daniel Brunskill played college football at San Diego State University. He signed with the Atlanta Falcons as an undrafted free agent on May 1, 2017.

Daniel Brunskill signed with the San Francisco 49ers. Brunskill reached Super Bowl LIV with the 49ers on April 11, 2019.
