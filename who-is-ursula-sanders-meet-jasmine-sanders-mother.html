Ursula Sanders Age, Job, Ethnicity

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, January 16, 2024

Ursula Sanders is the mother of Jasmine Sanders, a professional model and the star of My Unorthodox Life. Jasmine claims that her mom is her biggest supporter.

“I think my mom is one of my biggest fans. I cannot remember a day that has gone by that she is not rooting or screaming, or asking me what’s next or pushing me for the next big thing,” she told Ocean Drive. Jasmine referred to her mom as her “favorite person in the world” to Arcadia.

Hence, it is worth knowing more about the woman who births to her and supports her career. Find out everything there is to know where we cover her age, job, husband & kids, and more.

Meet Ursula Sanders, Jasmine Sanders Mother

My Unorthodox Life star Jasmine Sanders was born to her mother Ursula Sanders.

On a mother’s day post in 2020, she wrote: “Happy Mother’s Day Mommy!! Thanks for teaching me everything I know, traveling with me, waiting with me at castings, listening to me cry at night, and letting my lay my head on your stomach while you rub your hands through my hair.. I love you Mommy and can’t thank you enough for all of your support!”

She concluded the post, “Thanks for always keeping it real with me My Mommy and Forever Best Friend Can’t wait til I can come home and see you when everything opens back up! @ursulasandersmua @ Los Angeles, California.”

As reported, model Jasmine was also Elite World Group Virtual’s first avatar, the company that represented her.

Jasmine’s exotic looks aided in her thriving modeling career. She has been in the modeling business for a very long time and worked for several notable brands including Tommy Hilfiger, Fendi, and Bulgari. But, it wasn’t always like that for her.

Jasmine experienced racism-based bullying as a child at her school (her mother is white and her father is Black). “Living in the South, people look at you differently because you are a mixed kid. I would get bullied over who my friends were — if I had too many white friends or too many black friends,”  In 2018, she spoke to Ocean Drive.

At the age of 13, Jasmine joined her first modeling agency. Up until the age of 18, her mother accompanied her in every photo session and campaign.

Julia Haart wanted to go digital with the company when she worked at EWG. Julia intended to make famous people into avatars and introduce them to the Metaverse. Jasmine was the brand’s initial persona when the My Unorthodox Life star introduced the idea. In an interview with NBC News at the beginning of 2022, Julia claimed that “hyper-realistic avatars” could enable users to “be everywhere at once.”

Ursula Sanders Husband And Kids

Ursula Sanders met her husband Andre Sanders when Andre was serving in the military in Germany. The couple had Jasmine in Germany and later Ursula and Jasmine moved to Columbia, S.C. when Jas was around 1 year old. Jasmine has spoken about her father with Arcadia.

Jasmine said: “My dad is ex-military and he is a machine, he’s turned me into one too. When I was a kid, he used to say ‘it is a gift and a curse to be a girl.’ You’ve got to know how to protect yourself. He taught me to always be completely aware of my surroundings. Have fun at the club, but never let your guard down too much.”

“I appreciate my dad in so many ways, but especially for teaching me how to protect myself ahead of time,” she added more. “He taught me how to watch people like no other.”

By ethnicity, Andre is African-American and hailed from South Carolina. By profession, he was an ex-military. Jas described him as a machine. Born in 1960, Andre is 62 years old in April 2022.

Other than Jasmine, Andre and Ursula also have other two sons; Marco and Dominique, and a daughter named Alisha. Marco is currently 43 years old as he was born in August 1979. He is currently based in Oslo, Norway. Dominique is 26 years old and graduated from USC.

Andre turned celebrates his birthday on 27 May. On his 25th birthday, Ursula wished him: “Happy 25th Birthday to my son
Dominique. I love you very much.”

As for Alisha, she celebrates her birthday on 15 December.

Ursula Sanders Job

Ursula Sanders currently working as a freelance and regional makeup artist at Lancome Paris. She has been working in that role since June 2015. According to LinkedIn, before that, Ursula worked as a makeup artist at Trish McEvoy from June 2001 to March 2015. At the same time, he also worked as a counter manager at Darphin.

Ursula debuted as a makeup artist at Chanel in 1998. She worked at Chanel for two and a half years.
