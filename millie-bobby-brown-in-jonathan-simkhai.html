Millie Bobby Brown Looks All Grown Up In Jonathan Simkhai -

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

Millie Bobby Brown only entered the collective consciousness last year when "Stranger Things" became a national sensation. The Netflix series made stars out of its young cast quickly, but none more so than the talented wunderkind actress who bears a striking resemblance to her co-star, former child prodigy Winona Ryder. We've seen Brown make statements on the red carpet in feminine, age-approprate looks like overalls and dresses like a frothy white Red Valentino and floral Coach in the past.

Now that she's a certified teen, it appears Brown is experimenting with more grown-up ensembles—like this two-piece white skirt and asymmetrical top from designer Jonathan Simkhai. The actress, whose hair is still growing out from the buzz cut she sports on the series, balanced the fitted look with silver brogues. So while Eleven might be becoming a verifiable teen before our eyes, the overall effect is still, very, very cute.
