Biography

Alizee was born in Ajaccio, France on August 21, 1984. French pop artist best known for her Platinum-selling debut, the 2000 album Gourmandises. She has won and been nominated for many awards, including winning Single of the Week from NME for “Moi…Lolita.” Her song, “Moi…Lolita,” was featured in the 2006 film A Good Year, which starred Russell Crowe. She trained at the École de Danse Monique Mufraggi dance school from age four to fifteen.
