TikTok star 'Caleb Coffee' thanks God for surviving fall from Hawaii cliff

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, March 31, 2024

Caleb, also known as "Caleb Coffee" on TikTok, shared an update from a hospital bed while he recovers from a "60 to 80 foot fall from a Hawaii cliff.

"I just found out that my neck neck and spine is not broken, so thank you so much God," Caleb said from his hospital bed Thursday.

The 18-year-old social media star was airlifted after he slipped off a cliff and fell onto lava rock while on a hike with friends, stated family on gofundme.com.

I just woke up and thought I was going to die and then I didn't die," Caleb told over 11 million followers on TikTok.

Caleb is known for posting footage of doing stunts, working out or hanging out with friends and family.
