Starfield Sic Semper Tyrannis Leon Voclain Explained

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, June 6, 2024

Not sure what does Sic Semper Tyrannis mean in Starfield? One of the audio logs left by Leon Voclain in the Mantis mission in Starfield is titled Sic Semper Tyrannis. In it, he talks about the relationship with his mom, a meeting with his lawyer, and other details. In this guide, we explain the meaning behind this phrase, and why it is important in terms of gameplay.

What Does Sic Semper Tyrannis Mean in Starfield?

Starfield’s Sic Semper Tyrannis audio log can be found on the body of one of the spacers inside Mantis’ secret outpost. The audio file has been recorded by Leon Voclain, Mantis’ son, whose real name is Doriane Voclain. On it, he talks about a meeting with his mum after a long period and some other things. However, many players are confused by the title of the audio log. What does it mean and what does it have to do with this side quest? “Sic Semper Tyrannis” is a Latin phrase meaning “thus always to tyrants”. It essentially means that the rule of Tyrants always ends up in them being overthrown. And in the case of this side mission, the Tyrant is Doriane Voclain. Better known as the Mantis. Hence, Leon is speaking about his mother.

However, the cool Latin phrase is not there only for lore purposes. In fact, it’s also essential for completing this whole side quest. Near the end of the quest, you’ll reach a room with rows of letters on the ground. What you need to do here is to step on a specific letter in each row so that you create the word “TYRANNIS”.
