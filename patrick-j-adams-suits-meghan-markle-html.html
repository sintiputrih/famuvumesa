Patrick J. Adams to Don More Suits, Though Without Meghan Markle

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 30, 2024
Patrick J. Adams and Meghan Markle.

Despite the media circus two years ago that surrounded the departure of Suits leads Patrick J. Adams and Meghan Markle, the heart will always be wistful for those matching sets of outer clothes. And legal jargon! Variety reports that Adams has done an about-face and will return to the show’s final season for a few episodes, where his character, Mike, will get involved “in a case that will put him toe to toe with his former mentor Harvey (Gabriel Macht) and Samantha (Katherine Heigl).” We last saw Mike get married to the paralegal love of his life, Rachel (Meghan Markle), and move out West in newlywed bliss to open their own firm together. In reality, as Markle is now referred to as the Duchess of Sussex and is fully immersed in the “world” of “royal” “protocol,” we won’t waste your time in speculating if she’ll return. No, she won’t.
