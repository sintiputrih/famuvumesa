Travis Scott New Album Utopia 2023: Release Date, Tracklist, Features, Songs &amp; More!

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

Travis Scott is gearing up to release his highly anticipated fourth studio album, “Utopia.” The album has been teased for years, and fans are eagerly awaiting its release. In this article, you know more about the Travis Scott Utopia.

Travis Scott Utopia Release Date

The release date for “Utopia” is set for July 28, 2023. The album will be released through Scott’s Cactus Jack Records and Epic Records.

Travis Scott Utopia Tracklist

The tracklist for “Utopia” has not yet been released, but there have been a few singles released from the album. The singles include “Franchise” featuring Drake and Migos, “Escape Plan” featuring Future, and “K-POP” featuring Young Thug and ROSALÍA.

Travis Scott Utopia Features

In addition to the singles that have already been released, there are a number of other features rumored to be on “Utopia.” These features include Kanye West, Kid Cudi, The Weeknd, Beyoncé, and SZA.

Travis Scott Utopia Songs

The songs on “Utopia” are said to be a mix of genres, including hip-hop, trap, and R&B. The album is also said to be inspired by Scott’s love of nature and the outdoors.

Travis Scott Utopia Other Details

In addition to the music, “Utopia” will also be accompanied by a documentary film. The film will be directed by Teyana Taylor and will give fans a behind-the-scenes look at the making of the album.

Travis Scott Utopia Background

Travis Scott first announced “Utopia” in 2019, but the album was delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Scott has since teased the album on social media, and he has performed some of the songs from the album live.
